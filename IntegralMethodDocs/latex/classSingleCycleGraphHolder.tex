\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Holds all the graphs for all the single histograms of the program. EX\+: If we are doing 20 cycles of 20 runs for the decay chain 144Cs-\/$>$144Ba-\/$>$144La we have single graphs for each element in the decay chain and a total integral graph. The total integral graph for the 20 cycles of 20 runs are held by the Cylce\+Graph\+Holder class but the single graphs for Cs, Ba, and La are all held by this class.
