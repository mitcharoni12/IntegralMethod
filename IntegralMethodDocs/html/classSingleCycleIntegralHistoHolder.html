Detailed Description

Holds all the histograms for all the single integral histograms of the program. EX: If we are doing 20 cycles of 20 runs for the decay chain 144Cs->144Ba->144La we have single histograms for each element in the decay chain and a total integral and bateman histogram. The total integral and Bateman histograms for the 20 cycles of 20 runs are held by the CylceHistoHolder class but the single histograms for Cs, Ba, and La are all held by this class.
